Healthy tips:

Your health is the most important to us, so here are some healthy tips for you:



  • Base your meals on higher fibre starchy carbohydrates.
  • Cut down on saturated fat and sugar.
  • Eat less salt:no more than 6g a day for adults.
  • You need to drink a generous amount of water,try to avoid sugary and fizzy drinks.
  • Do not skip breakfast.
  • Choose whole grain bread instead of refined.
